Alba
Made from waste yoghurt pots

Alba is our best-selling material and you can see why
– it’s sophisticated enough that it wouldn’t look out of place in Selfridges or
Modern House, yet it’s also made from a humble kitchen cast-off: used yoghurt pots.

With its white, marble-like surface and hints of gold and silver (from yoghurt pot foil), Alba isn’t just easy on the eye. It’s easy to use too.

Available in 5mm, 12mm and 20mm thickness.

Panel Information

Every panel is unique, and colour and pattern may vary from sheet to sheet and from batch to batch. If you strictly need to minimise colour variation, discuss your needs with us prior to order to make sure all your material comes from the same batch.

We try very hard to make our sheets as blemish-free as we can, but they are not perfect; for example, they may have dimples and minor marks which are outside our control. Please contact us to discuss this if it could be a problem.

The listed board thicknesses are nominal: thermal expansion of some sheets can be 2mm per metre over a 10 degree (Celcius) range.

High Impact Polystyrene is a flammable material and should be placed at a sufficient distance from any naked flame.

MATERIAL PROPERTIES

  • The materials in our classics range have the following properties:
  • Hard, dense and rigid
  • 100% waterproof, rot-proof and strong weather resistance
  • Solid and consistent,allowing you to have a decorative edge
  • Moderate scratch resistance,and can be refinished
  • UV resistance(except our Alba material, which yellows in the sun but can be refinished)
  • Can be heat-formed into shapes
  • Matt/planed finish,and can be polished
  • Can be machined, drilled, cut (router, CNC), water cut and fixed using adhesives and screws
  • Custom Materials (router, CNC), water cut and fixed using adhesives and screws
