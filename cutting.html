Cutting and Finishing

We offer a cutting and fabrication service on all our materials from the production of semi-finished and finished parts to full product design and development.

We can work on any scale of project from prototyping and one-off commissions to small and larger batch production. Do get in touch to talk about your project.

Key services include:
  • Product design engineering / 3D CAD CAM
  • CNC cutting/drilling
  • Circular saw cutting
  • Finishing
  • Assembly of parts

CNC Routing

We have advanced 3-axis CNC machining capabilities in house offering you simple profile cutting and finished product manufacturing.

Laser-Cutting
