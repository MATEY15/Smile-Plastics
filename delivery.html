Delivery

Panels

For sheet orders it is usually 2-8 weeks from order to shipping, depending on stock levels. If we are cutting pieces, then it usually adds at least a week (more for cnc jobs than wall saw).

2000mmx1000mm pallet shipping in the UK is next day

3000mmx1200mm is a 2-3 day service.

TNT in the UK it is next day, and elsewhere it is usually under a week.

International pallets

It can be anywhere from a week to a couple of months depending on the shipping method and location (Ireland road freight is around a week, sea freight to China is months).

Samples

It is anywhere from next day to several weeks depending on the day they order and where it is going to.

*This is all excluding customs which are adding up to a week or two depending on the country and how fast the customer is at responding to import agents.
